## Employee Finder
** What does this app do?
This app is designed to allow you to take a survey and match up with an employee within the database in order to find a match close enough to you so you can work well with someone!

** How to use the app:
To start with this app you must answer a series of questions given to you by the survery. The algorithm will then decide who your match is based off of the responses given
